Candied Yams

Description

A very sweet and savory side dish to enjoy for the holidays. These coated yams will be one to remember.

Ingredients

  • 4 sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups brown sugar

Directions

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, place potatoes in water.
  2. Top with butter and sugar, cover and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer, without stirring, until mixture is thick and syrupy, for 40 minutes.
    • Mixture will thicken slightly as it cools.
  4. Remove from heat and serve warm
